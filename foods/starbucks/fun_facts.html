VIII. Why are Starbucks sizes called Tall, Grande, and Venti?

Show Answer

☕ Grande means "large" in Italian, and Venti means 20! The names were inspired by Italian coffee culture!

IX. Why does Starbucks have a marmaid logo?

Show Answer

☕ The famous logo is actually two-tailed siren, a mythical creature associated with sailors and the sea, fitting Starbucks' Seattle origins!

Show Answer

☕ Yes! In 2008, Starbucks temporarily closed more than 70,000 stores so baristas could receive additional training!
