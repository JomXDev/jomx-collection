The Age of Existence

Statistics

1987 💵

The Schultz Era

Howard Schultz purchased Starbucks and transformed it into a coffeehouse chain.

Espresso drinks became a major part of Starbucks culture.

Statistics
  • 17 stores existed worldwide
  • The CEO of Starbucks: Howard Schultz
  • Espresso drinks were introduced

1992 📈

NASDAQ Debut

Starbucks became a public company.

This allowed Starbucks to expand more rapidly

Statistics
  • 165 store exists
  • Listed on NASDAQ
  • Expansion accelerated

1996 🌏

International Expansion

Starbucks opened its first store outside North America

Japan became the first overseas market

Statistics
  • ~1,000 stores worldwide
  • Global Era Begins
  • Japan became Starbucks' first international market

2008 🎓

Training Day

Thousands of Starbucks stores temporarily closed so baristas could receive additional training

Starbucks sacrificed profit to improve coffee quality

Statistics
  • ~7,100 stores worldwide
  • Barista retraining
  • Focus on quality

2011 🧜‍♀️

The Modern Era

Starbucks introduced its fourth logo

The company removed the words "Starbucks Coffee" from the logo

Statistics
  • Strong global recognition
  • Fourth logo redesign
  • Strong global recognition

2024 🌎

The Current Status

Starbucks has become one of the largest coffee chains on Earth

Millions of customers visit Starbucks every week

Statistics
  • ~38,000 stores worldwide
